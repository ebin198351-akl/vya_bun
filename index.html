Vya's Kitchen 是什么？

What is Vya's Kitchen?

Vya's Kitchen 是一个从家里长出来的小厨房，用最简单的面粉、肉馅和蔬菜，做最安心的包子和饺子。

我们坚持手工和少量制作：当天和面，当天包制，当天出餐。不追求工业统一，只想把“家里包的”味道，稳稳地重复给你。

Vya's Kitchen started as a tiny home kitchen, turning simple flour, meat and veggies into comforting buns and dumplings.

We insist on small‑batch handmade production – kneaded, wrapped and cooked on the same day. Not factory‑perfect, just the taste of home you can always count on.

  • 每日限量，尽量提前预订
  • 严选肉类与新鲜蔬菜，馅心多汁不死咸
  • 支持冷冻生坯，回家想吃随时下锅
  • Limited quantity every day – pre‑order is recommended.
  • Carefully selected meat and fresh veggies, juicy but not too salty.
  • Frozen uncooked options are available for you to cook anytime.
